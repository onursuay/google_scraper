Yo Scraper

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Yo Scraper ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our application at scraper.yodijital.com.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Yo Scraper, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Google Account Data: When you connect your Google account via OAuth, we receive a temporary access token to read your Google Sheets files on your behalf. We do not store your Google credentials or refresh tokens.
  • Usage Data: We may collect basic usage information such as pages visited and features used, solely for improving the application.
  • Imported Data: Any data you import (Excel, CSV, or Google Sheets) is processed in memory and written to the Google Sheet you have configured. We do not retain copies of your imported data on our servers.

2. How We Use Google User Data

We use Google OAuth exclusively to allow you to browse and select your own Google Sheets files for import. Specifically:

  • We request read-only access to your Google Sheets and Drive metadata.
  • The access token is stored in a short-lived, httpOnly browser cookie and expires automatically.
  • We do not share, sell, or transfer your Google account data to any third party.
  • We do not use your Google data for advertising or any purpose beyond enabling the import feature.

3. Data Storage

Yo Scraper does not operate its own database for user data. All business data you collect or import is stored directly in the Google Sheet you connect. We do not retain personal data on our servers beyond the duration of your session.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google APIs – for Sheets and Drive access (OAuth 2.0)
  • Railway – for application hosting

5. Data Retention

Google OAuth tokens are stored in session cookies and expire within 1 hour. No personal data is retained on our servers after your session ends.

6. Your Rights

You may revoke Google account access at any time via Google Account Permissions. You can also disconnect your Google account directly from the application's External page.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@yodijital.com.
